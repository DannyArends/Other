<h2>BioSources.nl mission statement</h2>
Collaborate with other researchers to develop new bioinformatics tools & analysis, and providing an infrastructure for a 'shared work environment' for bioinformaticians. Currently we only provide some basic background information on bioinformatics, and links to other sites dedicated to bioinformatic research.
